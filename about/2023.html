About Colin

Who, me!? I'm a married, father of two living in Philadelphia, PA. I work full-time in front of a computer screen, but enjoy spending time with my family, taking in sporting events, and long chats with friends when AFK. In recent years, I've lost a bit of myself to parenting two beautiful (and crazy) kids, but bits of the old me shine through every so often.

I'm an east-coaster at heart, having been born outside of Philadelphia, growing up in New Jersey, and attending college in Delaware. I went west to San Francisco at the start of my career, but found my way back to the City of Brotherly Love in due time.

Along the way, I've kicked off a semi-regular olympiad of wacky sports, run my own software consultancy, helped found a tech startup, and balanced my career between remote and in-person work in many different contexts.

Personally, I married up in terms of brains and beauty, and created two amazing, small humans who keep me on my toes most days. If you need me, generally you can find me kicking around my neighborhood striving to become a regular at my favorite sandwich and coffee shops.
