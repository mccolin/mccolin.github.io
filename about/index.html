This is Colin McCloskey

Bearded computery nerd person. Philadelphian. Former beer review vlogger. Co-creator of Bennett.

About Colin

Who, me!? I'm a 34 year old, married, father of one living in Philadelphia, PA. I work full-time in front of a computer screen, but enjoy spending time with my family, taking in sporting events, and long chats with friends when AFK. I have little to no idea how to remodel a house, but just bought a big fixer-upper that needs some serious work.

I'm an east-coaster at heart, having been born outside of Philadelphia, growing up in New Jersey, and college'ing in Delaware. I went west to San Francisco at the start of my career, but found my way back to the City of Brotherly Love in due time.

Along the way, I've kicked off a semi-regular olympiad of wacky sports, run my own software consultancy, helped found a tech startup that published a patent, and tailored remote work for companies large and small.

Personally, I married up in terms of brains and beauty, and created an amazing, tiny human, who rules our roost with reckless abandon.

Most days, you can find me kicking around my neighborhood and taking in what life has to offer.
